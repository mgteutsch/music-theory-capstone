  • turned_in_notMore details about this page of the demo

    This page shows an example of an ear training exercise. For this demo, only one song will play ("Get Lucky" by Daft Punk), but the functionality still shows. Users can enter chords once the Play button is pressed, and they can click "Check Answer" to verify their chords. Correct answers will reveal blue, wrong answers will highlight in pink which chords are incorrect. The "Filter Chords" section is just a placeholder at this time. At full functionality, users can select only the chords they want to practice ear training with, and the app will play only songs clips that include the users' preferences.

    Also note: the "glow box" functionality is limited at this time; the code is being rewritten from a timing function in Javascript to a looped-timer in CSS to have continuity throughout the whole song clip.
