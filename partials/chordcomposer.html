  • turned_in_notMore details about this page of the demo

    This page was designed to satisfy the initial CRUD requirements for this project. Users can enter in major and minor chords (I, ii, iii, IV, V, vi, vii as well as i, II, III, iv, v, VI), and the player will light up the chord boxes as the respective sound files loop. Progressions can be saved for future logins, or edited, or deleted completely. Currently, there is nothing denoting what the user can and cannot type for valid chord progressions, and I plan to have limited options via a dropdown in a future release.
